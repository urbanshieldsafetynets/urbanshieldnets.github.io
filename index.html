About Urban Shield

Urban Shield Safety Nets is a leading provider of advanced fall protection solutions. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we are committed to delivering high-quality safety nets that meet international standards.

Our team of certified professionals ensures proper installation and maintenance of all our safety net systems, giving you peace of mind and complete protection.

Our mission is to safeguard lives with innovative netting solutions tailored for residential, commercial, and industrial needs. We use premium materials certified by ISO 9001 standards, ensuring durability and reliability.

From balcony safety nets to construction site protection, we’ve installed over 10,000 systems across India, earning trust through quality and service.
